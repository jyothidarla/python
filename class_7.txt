Slice operator with Negative direction:
======================================
Python supports slice operator with negative direction which starts
from -1 to end+1.While working with negative direction our step value
must be negative number otherwise most of the time empty is the output.

str1[-1:-7:-1] ---->-7+1=-6


Ex1:
===
import time 
str1="Core Python"
print(str1)
print()
print(type(str1))
print()
print("=====Slice operator using - direction====")
print(str1[-1:-7:-1])
print("========================")
print()
time.sleep(2)
print("End of an application")
